CHRISTIAN KETT

London based pop rock drummer

GET IN TOUCH

Christian is currently the drummer for UK band Prey Drive.

Having racked up nearly a million streams on their debut album ‘Once More With Feeling‘ – Prey Drive have opened for the likes of Busted and Fatherson as well as touring the UK and Europe.

Having previously sessioned with US rock band Slaves he is also no stranger to festival stages, playing to packed out tents at both Download and Graspop festivals.
